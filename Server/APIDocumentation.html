Get Array of Building Id’s and Names:

/api/buildings

{“buildings”: [{“b_id”: <id-(String)>, “name”: <name-(String)>}, … ]}

Get Array of every room in every building:

/api/rooms

{"buildings":[{"b_id":"X", "1":[ "XX",.. ],"floors":[ "1",.. ],"rooms":[ "XX",.. ]},..]}

Get dictionary of building rooms:

/api/rooms/b_id=X

{"b_id":"X", "1":[ "XX",.. ],"floors":[ "1",.. ],"rooms":[ "XX",.. ]}

Get Dictionary of Occupancies of all Buildings:

/api/locationinfo/buildings

{“occupancies”: {<b_id-(String)>:{“b_id”: <id-(String)>, “occupancy”: <occ-(int)>, “name”: <name-(String)>},...:}
